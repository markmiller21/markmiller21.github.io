Fit Friender

Like tinder but for workout partners

FitFriender is an app that connects users with their ideal workout partner based on workout activities (ie lifting, cycling, tennis) location, and gender preference. Users swipe through potential matches. If both users ‘like’ each other they are able to converse via a message terminal to setup a workout.

Basic responsive website for a fin-tech startup called EMpower

EMpower is an NYC based fin-tech startup. They are a development platform that partners with local organizations to build physical and energy infrastructure in emerging markets. Javascript racing game featuring Homer and Duffman

Simpsons Racer is simple racing game built qith HTML, CSS, JQuery, and Javascript. To move Homer and Duffman press the "Q" key and "P" key respectively. The winner will be determined and alerted when they reach the end of the screen
